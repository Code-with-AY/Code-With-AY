About FitStart

FitStart is designed for beginners who want to kickstart their fitness journey. Our 12-week program combines simple workouts, practical nutrition tips, and mindset strategies to help you build lasting habits. No gym required—just commitment and a desire to improve!

12-Week Beginner Program

Week 1-4: Foundation

Start with bodyweight exercises and basic nutrition planning. Focus on consistency.

Week 5-8: Build

Increase workout intensity and explore meal prep to sustain energy.

Week 9-12: Thrive

Incorporate new exercises and refine habits for long-term success.
